Grabo! - Connecting the world through food experiences

Grabo is a community-based app that helps users to get a sense of foreign food sold in the grocery stores by allowing them to scan the product and post it to the foreign community and ask questions about it.

Click to download the Grabo App on your mobile now!

Get Started

Features

There are many overseas food products that do not have sufficient product explanations in English or are described only in foreign languages.

We have all had the experience of refraining from buying food products from overseas because the packages are written in unreadable characters or because we do not know if it is a popular product or not.

We are deprived of the opportunity to experience foods from different countries for the first time as a result of this scenario.

Scan the product you like but know nothing about!

Let Grabo detect the language of the product!

And post it to the Grabo community!

Get advice within minutes from a local!
